## **Quick Start Guide (5 Minutes)**

### **Step 1: Open Vantom OS**
- Download and install Vantom OS app
- Login with your driver credentials
- Allow location and notification permissions

### **Step 2: Vehicle Check**
- Tap **"Start Vehicle Check"**
- Follow on-screen checklist
- Take photos of fuel level (automatic)
- Sign digital inspection form

### **Step 3: Start Route**
- Review your route for today
- Tap **"Start Route"** to begin deliveries
- Follow GPS navigation in app

### **Step 4: Monitor Dashboard**
- Check fleet status (other drivers nearby)
- Watch for safety alerts
- Monitor battery level

## **Troubleshooting**

### **App Not Working:**
- Check internet connection
- Restart app
- Clear cache if needed
- Contact support (built-in chat)

### **GPS Issues:**
- Ensure location permissions enabled
- Check GPS accuracy in settings
- Move to open area if needed
- Restart app if GPS stuck

### **Battery Draining:**
- Check battery optimization settings
- Close other background apps
- Ensure Vantom OS battery saver enabled
- Restart phone if needed

### **Emergency Not Working:**
- Check network connection
- Try manual rescue request
- Call Lead Driver as backup
- Use WhatsApp as emergency backup
